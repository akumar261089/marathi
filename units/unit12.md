### ЁЯУЦ Explanation

**Past tense** tells us:

- what **already happened**
- what someone **did before**

In Marathi, past tense works **differently from present tense**.

ЁЯСЙ **Golden Rule (Very Important):**

> In past tense,
> **Verb agrees with the OBJECT (karma)**,
> **not with the doer (karta)**.

ThatтАЩs why **рдиреЗ** becomes important.
